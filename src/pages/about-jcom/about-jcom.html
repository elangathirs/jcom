About JCI INDIA

Junior Chamber International (JCI) is an international organization founded in 1915 at St Louis, USA, and having its International Head Quarters at Chesterfield, St. Louis, USA. It is an NGO, imparting training to youth, developing them and transforms them as active citizen, for sustainable positive change around the world. It works with many organizations including UN, for sustainable development globally. JCI is spread in above 100 countries. Each Country is known as a National Organization Member (NOM). In India, JCI was launched in 1949. JCI India is the NOM affiliated to JCI, which is the international body. JCI India is a registered society under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 as amended by the Societies Registration Act (Punjab Amendment Act 1957) as extended to the State of Delhi.

About JCOM

JCOM is a Business area program exclusively for JCI members.The objective of the program is to develop the business opportunity of JCI members through networking.

Mission

To create and provide dynamic tools and development opportunities ensuring ethical business practices that nurture the exponential business growth of its members.

Vision

To create a business development platform for entrepreneurs of JCI fraternity to connect & grow locally and globally

Membership Oath

I do promise that I will faithfully serve the purposes of JCOM, and will be truthful with members and customers in providing the quality service by following the ethics of good business with the guidelines of JCOM

Officers Oath
